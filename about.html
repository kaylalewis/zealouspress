About

If you have an idea and want to share it with the world, come talk to us.

At Zealous, the outcome can be as lengthy as a novel or as bold as a billboard. Each project has its own identity and unique process. We love tackling new challenges, and use a variety of media, formats, and external sources to carry out our vision. Projects mean so much more when they reach the intended audience, so we strategize distribution, too.

We take our expertise and challenge it with each new project to come up with things that captivate people and inspire curiosity.

One day, we hope to help everyone share their story.

Send us your idea.





Who Are We?

We are an infinitely-curious team of artists, writers, designers, and engineers. And no, each of us does not assume one role: we are all multifaceted. We like it that way.
